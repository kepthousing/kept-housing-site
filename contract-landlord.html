Listing Agreement

1. Nature of Services

Kept Housing for All ("Provider") provides housing connection and listing support services. Provider is not a licensed real estate broker, real estate agent, property management company, attorney, or financial advisor. All services are informational, organizational, and connective in nature only.

2. Listing Services

Provider will make reasonable efforts to share the landlord's listing with individuals seeking housing, facilitate introductions between the landlord and prospective tenants, and assist with general communication. Provider does not guarantee that a tenant will be found or that any prospective tenant will meet the landlord's specific requirements.

3. Landlord Responsibilities

The landlord is solely responsible for screening and selecting tenants, complying with all applicable federal, state, and local fair housing laws, drafting and executing any lease or rental agreement, and maintaining the property in a habitable condition as required by law. Provider plays no role in the landlord-tenant legal relationship.

4. No Tenant Screening

Provider does not perform background checks, credit checks, employment verification, or any form of formal tenant screening. The landlord is solely responsible for conducting any screening they deem necessary before entering into a lease agreement.

5. Fair Housing

The landlord agrees to comply with all applicable fair housing laws, including the Fair Housing Act, and not to discriminate against any prospective tenant based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, disability, or any other protected class under applicable law.

6. Flat Fee & Payment

Listing and connection services are provided for a flat fee agreed upon before services begin. The fee is for Provider's connection services only and is not contingent on a successful lease being signed.

7. Confidentiality

Provider will keep landlord information confidential and will not share contact details with third parties except as necessary to facilitate introductions with prospective tenants at the landlord's direction.

8. Limitation of Liability

Provider's liability is limited to the flat fee paid. Provider is not liable for any tenant behavior, property damage, lease disputes, or any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina.
