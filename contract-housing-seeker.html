Provider will make reasonable efforts to identify available housing options, provide general guidance on the rental application process, facilitate introductions between the client and landlords, and offer relocation planning support where applicable. Provider acts as a support resource and advocate, not as a licensed agent.

Provider makes no guarantee of housing placement, approval by any landlord, employment outcomes, or any other specific result. All outcomes depend on factors outside Provider's control including landlord decisions, availability, and client qualifications.

The client is solely responsible for reviewing, evaluating, and signing any lease, rental agreement, or other legal document. Provider strongly recommends that clients consult a qualified legal professional before signing any binding agreement. Provider is not a party to any lease or rental agreement between the client and a landlord.

Services are provided for a flat fee agreed upon before services begin. The fee covers the scope of services discussed at intake. No refund is guaranteed once services have commenced, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Provider will keep client information confidential and will not sell or share personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the agreed services (e.g., sharing contact information with a landlord at the client's direction).
