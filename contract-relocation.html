Service Agreement

1. Nature of Services

Kept Housing for All ("Provider") provides relocation support and housing connection services. Provider is not a licensed real estate broker, real estate agent, property management company, employment agency, attorney, or financial advisor. All services are informational, organizational, and connective in nature only.

2. Relocation Support Services

Based on the services selected by the client, Provider will make reasonable efforts to: identify available housing options in the client's target area; provide a personalized step-by-step moving plan; offer general job search direction and resources; arrange virtual video tours of neighborhoods and areas of interest; identify local hobbies, activities, and community resources; and compile a local resource directory for the client's destination area. Provider acts as a support resource and personal advocate — the client remains responsible for all final decisions.

3. No Guarantees

Provider makes no guarantee of housing placement, employment, or any other specific outcome. All results depend on factors outside Provider's control including availability, landlord decisions, employer decisions, and the client's individual qualifications and circumstances. Job search direction is general guidance only and does not constitute employment placement services.

4. Client Responsibility

The client is solely responsible for reviewing, evaluating, and signing any lease, rental agreement, employment contract, or other legal document. Provider strongly recommends consulting qualified legal or professional advisors before signing any binding agreement. Provider is not a party to any lease, rental agreement, or employment agreement.

5. Video Tours

Virtual video tours are informational only and are not a substitute for an in-person visit. Provider does not guarantee that any property, neighborhood, or area shown will be available or suitable for the client's needs at the time of their move.

6. Flat Fee & Payment

Services are provided for a flat fee agreed upon before services begin. The fee covers the scope of services selected and discussed at intake. No refund is guaranteed once services have commenced, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

7. Confidentiality

Provider will keep client information confidential and will not sell or share personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the agreed services.

8. Limitation of Liability

Provider's liability is limited to the flat fee paid. Provider is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of its services, including but not limited to damages related to housing, employment, or relocation decisions.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina.
