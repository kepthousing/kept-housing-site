What We Do

Kept Housing for All connects people who need housing with landlords who have available rental properties. We simplify the process and help both sides move forward smoothly.

How We Operate

  • We connect individuals with available housing opportunities.
  • We do not own, lease, or manage properties.
  • We do not collect rent or handle payments.
  • All lease agreements are directly between tenant and landlord.
