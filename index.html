What we offer

Services built around your needs

Whether you need housing, are a landlord with a vacancy, or you're relocating to a new city — we have support for you.

For People Seeking Housing

We help you identify available housing options and connect you with landlords — simplifying a stressful process.

  • Help identifying available rental options
  • Guidance on application processes
  • Connection and introductions to landlords
  • Support understanding lease expectations
  • We advocate for you throughout the process

For Landlords

We help you find qualified prospects and fill vacancies. Post your listing and let us handle the matchmaking.

  • Listing your available property on our platform
  • Matching with people actively seeking housing
  • Assistance filling vacancies faster
  • Simple communication facilitation

Full Relocation Support

Moving to a new area is a lot. We plan, organize, and guide you so you can settle in without the overwhelm.

  • Step-by-step personalized moving plan
  • Finding potential housing in your new location
  • Job search direction and resources
  • Local hobbies and community interest discovery
  • Virtual video tours of neighborhoods
  • Local area resource directory

Schools, Universities & Daycares

Whether you have children, are a student, or need childcare — we help you find the right educational fit in your area.

  • Research local public and private schools
  • Identify nearby colleges and universities
  • Find licensed daycare and childcare options
  • Virtual tours of school neighborhoods
  • General enrollment guidance and resources

Senior Living & Care

Helping seniors and their families find safe, comfortable living and care options — with patience and compassion.

  • Research independent and assisted living communities
  • Identify memory care and nursing facilities
  • Find in-home care and support resources
  • Virtual tours of senior communities
  • Advocate and communicate on your family's behalf

Important: What We Are & What We're Not

Kept Housing for All is an independent housing connection and relocation support service. We are not a licensed real estate broker, real estate agent, property management company, or legal or financial advisor.

We provide informational guidance, organizational support, and connection services only. We do not negotiate leases or contracts on your behalf, guarantee housing placement or employment, and we are not responsible for decisions made by landlords, employers, or other third parties.

All final decisions — including signing any rental agreement or lease — are made solely by you. We strongly encourage you to review any documents with a qualified professional before signing.
