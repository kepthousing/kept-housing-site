About Kept Housing for All

A calm, safe home is the foundation for everything else.

Our mission

Kept Housing for All exists to provide safe, stable co-living homes for people who need more than just a room — they need a community. We currently focus on:

  • Veterans transitioning into or within civilian life
  • Independent seniors wanting a calm, community-style home
  • Responsible adults who value safety, respect, and stability
How we’re different
  • Small, home-like environments — not large facilities.
  • Clear house guidelines to protect every resident’s safety.
  • Thoughtful matching of residents for a peaceful environment.
  • Transparent, straightforward pricing — no hidden fees.

Our Co-Living Communities

Designed for people who want independence with the comfort of shared space.

Veterans Co-Living Homes

Stable housing and a sense of belonging for those who have served.

  • Furnished private bedrooms with shared common areas.
  • Quiet, respectful homes with clear house standards.
  • Support coordination with veteran organizations.

Retirement Co-Living Homes

For independent, active older adults seeking peace, safety, and community.

  • Easy-access or single-story homes when possible.
  • Low-maintenance living with included utilities.
  • Optional light social activities.

Future Housing Options

Expanding soon to additional responsible working adults.

  • Co-living for nurses, caregivers, and essential workers.
  • Stable housing for vetted gig-economy workers.
  • More locations as communities grow.

Safety & Resident Standards

Everyone deserves to feel safe where they sleep.

Screening & house rules
  • Background and safety-focused resident screening.
  • Written guidelines for guests, quiet hours, and shared spaces.
  • No-tolerance policies for violence or illegal activity.
  • Collaboration with caseworkers when appropriate.
Home standards
  • Clean, regularly maintained homes.
  • Functional furnishings and essentials.
  • Safety checks and emergency info.
  • Focus on stability and consistency.

How It Works

Simple steps to find out if one of our homes is a good fit.

  1. Reach out. Email or call with your situation.
  2. Quick conversation. Discuss move-in date, budget, needs.
  3. Application & screening. Fill out application + checks.
  4. Home match. Receive a room offer if it's a good fit.
  5. Move in. Sign agreement, receive keys, get settled.
